DAILY TIP
Humming for 2 minutes before bed can help promote nasal breathing by producing nitric oxide.
The Science Behind This
Humming increases nitric oxide production in the nasal sinuses by up to 15x. Nitric oxide is a vasodilator that helps open nasal passages and improves oxygen absorption in the lungs.
