The Science Behind Mouth Taping
Mouth taping during sleep is a practice that encourages nasal breathing by gently keeping the mouth closed. Research suggests this can improve sleep quality and reduce snoring.
"Nasal breathing filters, warms, and humidifies air, which is optimal for lung health."
Studies show that consistent nasal breathing during sleep leads to better oxygen saturation and deeper sleep cycles.
